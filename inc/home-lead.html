    Led by Brazilian businesswoman and journalist Carla Bensoussan, Lead Hub, a leading strategic communication and public relations in Brazil, is headquartered in Recife (PE), with units in Rio de Janeiro (RJ) and São Paulo (SP) - and now Miami (USA).
    There are more than 950 clients served, 2,000 suppliers and 1,500 actions carried out in the Brazil. Since its foundation in 2005, Lead has been at the forefront of communication that acts and thinks differently, and that evolves according to market changes.
